All Ingredients

Ceramide NP Skin Barrier Safe ▼
Ceramides are naturally occurring lipids found in skin. Ceramide NP helps restore and maintain the skin's barrier function. Widely considered safe and beneficial.
Hyaluronic Acid Humectant Safe ▼
A naturally occurring polysaccharide that holds up to 1000x its weight in water. Excellent hydrating ingredient with a strong safety profile.
Niacinamide Brightening Safe ▼
Vitamin B3 derivative. Helps improve skin tone, reduces pore appearance, and has anti-inflammatory properties. Well-tolerated by most skin types.
Cetearyl Alcohol Emulsifier Caution ▼
A fatty alcohol (not drying). Generally safe but can cause reactions in sensitive skin individuals. Not related to drying alcohols like ethanol.
Methylparaben Preservative Avoid ▼
Synthetic preservative. Classified as an endocrine disruptor. Has been detected in breast tissue samples. Banned in some countries for leave-on cosmetics.
Glycerin Humectant Safe ▼
A naturally derived moisturizing ingredient. Attracts water to the skin's surface. One of the safest and most studied ingredients in cosmetics.
